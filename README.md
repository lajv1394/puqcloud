## WHMCS Modules: What They Do and What They Cost

This is where most IT providers will spend their time. PUQcloud organizes their WHMCS modules into four categories: Clouds, Docker, VPN, and Media. All modules support 25+ languages and come with a **7-day grace period** (basically a risk-free trial window).

### Cloud Modules

The flagship here is the **Proxmox KVM module** — probably their most feature-rich product. It handles full KVM lifecycle management directly from WHMCS without ever touching the Proxmox panel. Auto VM creation, suspend/unsuspend, termination, dual-disk provisioning, cloud-init configuration, noVNC console, snapshot and backup management, OS reinstall, real-time CPU/RAM/disk/network graphs, IPv4/IPv6 configuration, and per-VM firewall anti-spoofing rules. For anyone selling VPS services, this is serious automation for a very reasonable price.

Other cloud modules cover Nextcloud, ownCloud, HestiaCP, Hetzner Datacenter, PowerDNS, ReverseDNS, MinIO S3, and Synology.

👉 [Get WHMCS Module Proxmox KVM](https://puqcloud.com/cart.php?a=add&pid=13&aff=13)

### VPN Modules

The WireGuard VPN module pairs with PUQVPNCP to let you sell VPN accounts at scale. Auto-provisioning, bandwidth limits, suspend/unsuspend/terminate, and client-area connection status. The Business-VPN variant adds features suited to enterprise clients who need to manage their own configurations. Mikrotik variants are available for those running Mikrotik-based infrastructure.

👉 [Get WHMCS Module WireGuard VPN](https://puqcloud.com/cart.php?a=add&pid=33&aff=13)

### Docker Modules

A newer category that's been expanding quickly: n8n, Grafana, InfluxDB, NextCloud (Docker), Immich, and MinIO. These let you sell containerized service instances directly through WHMCS, which is great for adding monitoring-as-a-service or self-hosted automation tools to your portfolio.

### Media Modules

Jellyfin and Emby modules (both standard and Family variants) let you sell managed media streaming accounts. Clients can manage their own devices and libraries; admins get full suspend/terminate/password controls.
